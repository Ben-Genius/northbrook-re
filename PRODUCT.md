# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Two overlapping audiences arrive at this site:

1. **International E&P operators** — procurement and ops teams at companies like Eni, Saipem, Fugro, and Borr Drilling, based in Europe, Asia, or the Gulf, vetting a West Africa logistics partner before committing a vessel call or cargo consignment. They scan for certifications, track record, and whether this company can actually perform under pressure.

2. **Regional maritime and freight buyers** — shipping agents, freight managers, and vessel operators based in Ghana and West Africa who know the market but need to confirm North-Brook is the right call for their specific job, whether that's bunkering, husbandry, or crew management.

Both audiences are evaluating operational credibility, not reading marketing copy. They want evidence, not promises.

## Product Purpose

North-Brook Ltd. is a Ghanaian oil and gas logistics company providing integrated ship agency, freight forwarding, bunkering, husbandry, crew management, and warehousing services. Established 2011/2014, certified by ABS, ClassNK, DNV-GL, Lloyd's Register, RINA, and Korean Register.

This site exists to convert credibility into inquiries. A visitor should leave either having contacted the ops desk or having no remaining doubt that North-Brook is the right partner for West Africa operations. Success means the site closes deals that would otherwise go to an international competitor with less local knowledge.

## Brand Personality

**Authoritative, Precise, Formidable.**

North-Brook speaks from a position of deep operational experience, not aspiration. The voice is confident, technical, and efficient — every sentence drives toward operational clarity. No fluff, no generic superlatives. The company has moved ENI vessels and managed DNV-GL inspections; the site should feel like it comes from an organization that already knows the answer before you ask.

Tone: industry-insider authority. Not loud, not soft. The confidence of a 24/7 ops desk that picks up on the second ring.

## Anti-references

- **Generic SaaS blue**: Stripe-era rounded cards, feature icon grids, light gradients. Looks like a startup, not an operator.
- **Heavy industrial brutalism**: All-caps, thick borders, warehouse orange. Rugged but not credible at the Eni/Saipem level.
- **Slick agency minimalism**: Full-bleed photography, whisper-grey text, 1px rules. Feels like a design studio, not a company that moves cargo.
- **Government/compliance drab**: Navy + gold seals, serif-heavy layouts. Safe, forgettable, and does not differentiate.

The site must feel like none of these. It is a premium, industrial-grade operator's presence: dark, structured, measured, and undeniably serious.

## Design Principles

1. **Evidence over claims.** Certifications, vessel names, client logos, operational numbers — show proof before adjectives. Every design decision should surface credibility signals, not decorative interest.

2. **Operator precision.** Spacing, typography, and layout should feel calibrated, not expressive. The site is well-engineered the same way a cargo manifest is well-engineered: nothing misplaced.

3. **Weight without noise.** Dark surfaces carry authority without requiring excessive decoration. Let the depth of the background do the work; reserve the red accent for true calls to action and key data points.

4. **Motion like machinery.** Animations are deliberate, heavy, and purposeful — GSAP scroll entrances that feel like a crane lowering a container, not a bounce or a flutter. Nothing animates for decoration.

5. **Local confidence, global legibility.** The copy and design must read as credible to an international E&P procurement team AND feel deeply rooted in West African operational context. Both at once, without hedging.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AAA target. All text contrast ratios at AAA thresholds. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` (already wired into GSAPProvider). No information conveyed by color alone. Focus states visible and on-brand (accent ring, already defined in globals.css). All interactive elements keyboard-navigable.
