# North-Brook Ltd. Agent Guidelines

This document outlines the core brand identity, design principles, and success indicators for North-Brook Ltd. logic and content generation.

## 1. Brand Voice & Personality

The North-Brook voice is **authoritative, precise, and operationally focused**. We are experts who thrive in high-stakes environments where others hesitate.

### Core Principles
- **Authoritative & Expert**: Use confident, industry-standard terminology. Speak from a position of deep West African logistical experience.
- **Precise & Efficient**: Avoid fluff. Every sentence should drive towards operational clarity. "Precision in Oil & Gas" isn't just a slogan; it's the standard.
- **Bold but Reliable**: Use phrases like "where others hesitate" and "moving entire operations forward." However, always ground this boldness in safety and compliance.
- **Human-Centric Reliability**: Balance the industrial tone with "delivering trust at every turn." We are a partner, not just a service provider.

### Do's & Don'ts
- **DO**: Use words like *Precision*, *Integrated*, *Compliance*, *Agility*, *Track Record*, *Sub-region*.
- **DO**: Highlight our "local insight with global expertise."
- **DON'T**: Use generic marketing jargon ("unparalleled," "best-in-class") without specific context.
- **DON'T**: Sound overly casual. Maintain a professional, industrial-grade tone.

## 2. Design Tokens & Aesthetics

The visual identity reflects the industrial, maritime, and oil & gas sectors we serve. It is clean, high-contrast, and "Heavy-Duty."

### Color Palette
- **North-Brook Red**: Primary accent. Vibrant, industrial red. Symbolizes energy, action, and urgency.
- **Charcoal Gray / Deep Navy**: Primary background/text colors. Reflects stability, machinery, and professionalism.
- **Pure White**: For clarity and contrast.
- **Success Green**: Used sparingly for compliance and QHSE-related indicators.

### Typography
- **Headings**: Bold, high-impact Sans-Serif (e.g., Inter, Roboto, or similar industrial fonts). Should feel technical and sturdy.
- **Body**: Highly legible Sans-Serif. optimized for technical documentation and service descriptions.

### Imagery & Micro-interactions
- **Imagery**: Authentic, high-resolution industrial photography (offshore rigs, bulk cargo, ship husbandry, warehouses). Avoid generic stock photos.
- **Animations**: Use GSAP for smooth, "heavy" transitions. Movement should feel like well-oiled machinery—deliberate and high-performance.

## 3. Success Metrics & Track Record

These are the key indicators of North-Brook’s market leadership and reliability. Use these to build credibility in copy and components.

### Operational Scale
- **Experience**: Over 11 years of hands-on experience (Established 2011/2014).
- **Service Lines**: Integrated suite including Air/Sea Freight, Bunkering, Husbandry, Warehousing, and Crew Management.
- **Regional Reach**: Strong focus on Ghana and the West African sub-region.

### Certifications & Standards (Crucial)
- **Multi-Certified**: ABS, ClassNK, DNV-GL, Lloyd’s Register, RINA, Korean Register.
- **Safety**: Zero compromise on QHSE timelines and compliance.

### High-Profile Clients & Projects
- **Partners**: Eni, Saipem, Fugro, Borr Drilling, OSM Thome, Dolphin Geo Subsea.
- **Vessel Calls**: Safeen Argus 2, Luna Nelle, Ametrin Valor.

## 4. Agent Objectives
When generating content or code for North-Brook, the goal is to **WOW the user with a premium, industrial-grade experience** that feels both technologically advanced and operationally grounded.
