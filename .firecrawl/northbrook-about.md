But beyond the technical capabilities, what sets us apart is our responsiveness, regional expertise, and a reputation for solving complex logistical challenges with precision and flexibility

Who We are

# North-Brook Limited

For over a decade, North-Brook Limited has powered some of the most demanding logistics operations across Ghana and the West African sub-region. Whether it’s about delivering vital offshore equipment to rigs off the coast of Takoradi, coordinating multi-modal cargo to remote inland locations, or managing ship agency services under tight maritime deadlines, you can trust North-Brook Limited’s strong track record.

CSR

# CORPORATE SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY

At North-Brook Limited, we believe true success is measured not only in business performance but also in the positive impact we leave on society and the environment. Our CSR efforts focus on practical, high-impact initiatives that support education, community well-being, environmental protection, and capacity building.

## Our Community Impact

Education & Skills Development

Donation of books, computers, and school supplies to coastal community schools.

Community Support

Sponsorship of local sports events that promote youth development and discipline.

Distribution of basic PPE (gloves, helmets, masks) to local contractors and artisans.

Environmental Protection

Tree planting projects to reduce carbon impact and restore green spaces.

Promotion of waste segregation and recycling practices within our warehouses and

partner facilities.

Local Employment & Capacity Building

Partnerships with vocational institutions to develop a pipeline of skilled technicians.
