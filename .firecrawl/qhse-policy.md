Quality, Health, Safety and Environment Policy Statement

NORTH-BROOK LIMITED is committed to operating responsibly and in all its activities, account is taken of the need to protect the health and safety of its employees and other involved persons, provide security and conserve the environment, whilst providing the customers and stakeholders with a competent, safe, environmentally sound and cost-effective service which meets and exceeds industry specifications for QHSE. management.

NORTH-BROOK LIMITED aims to eliminate work-related injuries and illnesses and achievehigh environmental protection standards by following a zero tolerance for environmentalpollution. NORTH-BROOK complies with the purpose and spirit of relevant legislation andconventions, aiming to protecthealth, safety, and the environment, and provide security for alland avoid property damage

In this regard, NORTH-BROOK LIMITED  will:

- 1. Will comply with mandatory rules, regulations, and take into account codes, guidelines and standards from maritime organisations in managing vessels.

- 2. Actively promote high standards of safety consciousness, discipline and individual accountability. It encourages employees' involvement in defining and monitoring measures taken to safeguard and improve health and safety at work.

- 3. Promote satisfaction of Customers and stakeholders wherever possible by meeting and exceeding their needs and expectations

- 4. Strive to efficiently and effectively prevent pollution, prevent occupational injury and ill health, optimise energy and resource consumption and minimise the environmental impacts from activities, services and products.

- 5. Regularly assess and continually improve EHS performance responsibly by implementing management systems, setting goals, and meeting objectives.

- 6. Appropriately train, inform, motivate and consult with employees to help them perform their activities in a safe and environmentally responsible manner.

- 7. Promote the adoption of similar principles by contractors and suppliers.


This policy will be regularly reviewed, updated as necessary, applied and communicated to all employees and persons working for or on behalf of NORTHBROOK, and made available to interested parties and the public.
