Who we are

# North-Brook Ltd.

Established in November 2014, a leading Ghanaian company specialises in total Onshore and Offshore logistics Solutions. Our mandate offers a combined suite of services, including Air and Sea Freight, Cargo Consolidation, Ship Agency and Husbandry, Crew Management, Bunkering, and Warehousing.

[About Us](https://northbrook.com.gh/about-us/)
